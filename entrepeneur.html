Smart Sprinkler

The Smart Sprinkler is an invention that is simple in theory but complex in creation. The smart sprinkler will outdoo any existing sprinkler system in price, ease of use, and effectiveness. What makes the Smart sprinkler so effective is its ability to conserve precious water, its high tech properties, and its ease of use and installation

The smart sprinkler is a money saving machine. It will help you save on your water bill. The days of over watering, watering the sidewalk, and uneven watering are gone. The smart sprinkler is a system that anyone can set up. Simply connect your hose and place in the middle of your yard/garden. Its gps system allows you to program the exact proportions of your yard/garden in to make sure no extra water is being used. Finally, what is most appealing is smart sprinkler ability to self manage. connecting to local wifi board in the house, users will have the ability to create patterns when sprinkling will happen. There will also be an app that allows users to change when they want to water and even the frequency of watering. It would give updates on weather patterns, and other tidbits of useful info.
